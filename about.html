Hazel Yoga

Hazel offers Vinyasa Flow yoga classes in the Cheshire area, based around Congleton and Macclesfield. Open classes and private sessions are available, please email for details and pricing.

Vinyasa Flow is a dynamic and engaging style of yoga that links breath to movement in flowing sequences. Using meditation and breathing techniques, it provides an all-encompassing practice that unites mind, body and spirit.

Hazel is passionate about yoga, and the profound effects it can have on both physical and mental wellbeing. She is extremely fortunate to have been trained by the renowned Claire Missingham, and is inspired to deliver engaging and exciting classes. When she isn't teaching, she is dedicated to her own yoga practice - deepening her knowledge and experience through reading, attending classes and workshops.
